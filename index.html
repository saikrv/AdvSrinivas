About Counsel

A practice built on preparation, not promises

S

For over fifteen years, Advocate Srinivasa Rao has appeared before the Hyderabad District and Sessions Courts, the Family Court, and the Telangana High Court, building a practice on thorough drafting, direct client communication, and careful preparation before every hearing.

The practice covers civil disputes and recovery suits, criminal defence at every stage from bail to appeal, cheque-bounce matters under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, and family law — including divorce, maintenance, custody, and domestic violence protection. Every matter, large or small, is handled with the same attention.

Consultations are conducted in Telugu, Hindi and English, and clients are kept informed in plain language at each stage of their case — not left to decode legal paperwork alone.

How a Matter Proceeds

From first meeting to final resolution

Step 01

Initial Consultation

The case is discussed in detail, documents are reviewed, and an honest first assessment is given.

Step 02

Case Assessment

A clear legal opinion, likely course of action, and fee structure are set out before any filing begins.

Step 03

Filing & Representation

Pleadings are drafted, filed, and the matter is represented at every hearing before the concerned court.

Step 04

Resolution

Through settlement, judgment, or — where warranted — appeal, the client is kept informed at every step.
